Daily Reflection

A moment of God's Word for your day.

Friday, July 10, 2026

Today's Bible Verse

The Lord is my shepherd; I shall not want. — Psalm 23:1

Father Peter's Reflection

In the quiet of each day, the Lord leads us beside still waters. He restores our souls, guides us along right paths, and walks with us even through the darkest valleys.

When we feel lost or weary, He is the shepherd who never leaves us. Let us trust His gentle guidance today, and rest in the peace of His presence.

To be led by the Good Shepherd is to be free — free from fear, free from anxiety, free to love as He loves.

Closing Prayer:
Lord Jesus, Good Shepherd of my soul, guide my steps today. Keep me close to Your heart, and let me rest in Your peace. Where I am afraid, give me courage. Where I am weary, give me rest. Amen.
